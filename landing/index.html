Features

What Solar AI Does

A personal AI that lives on your machine, organized around the way you actually think and work.

Your AI, Always On

Runs locally 24/7. No internet required for core features. Your AI is always available, ready when you are.

Planet-Based Workspaces

Organize your life into planets. Each planet keeps its own memory, context, and history. Work stays separate from Health.

Gmail Integration

Read, search, and summarize emails. Ask your AI to draft replies or find that invoice from last month.

Calendar Awareness

Connects to Google Calendar. Get daily briefings, schedule events, and never miss a meeting again.

Web Search & Fetch

Your AI can search the web and read any URL to give you up-to-date answers grounded in real sources.

Shell Access

Run terminal commands through your AI. Automate tasks, manage files, write and execute scripts.

Interface

Your Universe, One Screen

Click any planet to open its context. Your AI remembers everything about each domain.
