Features

Everything orbits
around you

A personal AI operating system that organizes your world into planets — each one a project, a goal, a life area. Your AI sun connects them all.

AI Sun — Your Central Intelligence

Powered by Claude, your AI sun reads emails, manages tasks, searches the web, and runs commands — all through natural conversation. It knows your planets and acts accordingly.

Planet Projects

Every project gets its own planet. Tasks, notes, conversations — all contained in its own orbit.

Gmail Integration

Read, search, and summarize your emails. Your AI understands your inbox and acts on it.

Calendar Sync

See today's events, create new ones, and let your AI brief you every morning.

100% Local & Private

Your data never leaves your machine. SQLite database stored locally. API keys in your system keychain. No tracking, no cloud sync, no compromises. You own everything.
