## What is Solar AI OS?

Most AI tools are just chat boxes. Solar AI OS is different.

Your life is organized as a **solar system** — you are the sun, and each area of your life is a planet orbiting you. School. Work. Health. Sports. Finance. Each planet has its own persistent memory and AI context.

Click a planet → get an AI that actually knows what's going on in that area of your life.

No context switching. No re-explaining yourself. No subscription. No one reading your data.

## Privacy First

> **Solar AI physically cannot access your data — because we never see it.**

Solar AI runs entirely on your own [Claude API key](https://console.anthropic.com). Your conversations, your memory, your planets — all stored locally. We have no servers receiving your personal data. This isn't a privacy policy. It's the architecture.

## Features

- 🌍 **Solar System UI** — Your life visualized as an interactive, animated solar system
- 🪐 **Life Domain Planets** — Create planets for any area of your life (Work, School, Health, Finance, Sports...)
- 🧠 **Persistent Memory** — Each planet remembers your context across conversations
- 📬 **Gmail + Google Calendar** — Connected and context-aware
- 📰 **Live News** — News planet pulls current events into your AI context
- 💬 **Streaming Responses** — Real-time AI responses, no waiting
- 🚀 **Mission System** — Launch new missions from the command center
- 🖥️ **Desktop + Web** — Native desktop app (Windows) + web version

## Quick Start

### Web Version (Instant, no install)
👉 [solar-ai-three.vercel.app](https://solar-ai-three.vercel.app)

### Desktop App (Windows)
Download the latest installer from [Releases](https://github.com/theintelligentmind00-droid/Solar-Ai/releases).

### Setup (2 minutes)
1. Get a free API key at [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com)
2. Open Solar AI OS
3. Paste your key → done

That's it. Your solar system is live.
