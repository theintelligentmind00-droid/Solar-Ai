## Substack / Beehiiv Newsletter

### Issue 1 — The Origin Story
**Subject:** Why I built an AI that works like a solar system

```
Hi,

I want to tell you the real story behind Solar AI OS.

Not the polished version. The actual one — with the decisions, the mistakes, and the moments where it almost didn't happen.

**The frustration**

I've been using AI assistants for a while now. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini. They're all incredible. And they all have the same fundamental problem:

They don't know me.

Every conversation starts from zero. I explain who I am, what I'm working on, what I care about. And the next day — blank slate. It's like having the world's most capable assistant with permanent amnesia.

Worse: my life has structure. I'm building a startup AND trying to stay healthy AND managing finances AND maintaining relationships. These aren't the same thing. They don't belong in the same conversation.

**The idea**

What if your AI organized itself around your life instead of forcing you to organize around it?

That's where the solar system came from. Not as a visual gimmick — as a product philosophy.

You are the Sun. Everything orbits around you.

Each domain of your life is a planet. Work. Health. Finance. Travel. Whatever matters to you. Each planet has its own memory, its own history, its own context. They never bleed into each other.

**What we built**

Solar AI OS is a local-first personal AI desktop app. Here's what that means:

- **Local-first:** Your data never leaves your machine. SQLite database. No cloud. No telemetry.
- **Planet-based workspaces:** Each planet remembers everything you've discussed in that domain.
- **Rich memory system:** After every conversation, Solar extracts what's worth remembering — your goals, your preferences, the people in your life, your patterns — and uses them naturally going forward.
- **Real tools:** Gmail, Google Calendar, web search, file reading, shell access. Your AI can actually do things.
- **Streaming responses:** No waiting. Text appears as it's generated.

**The hard parts**

I'll be honest: packaging this was brutal. Getting a Python AI backend bundled into a Windows installer that just works on a fresh machine, with no terminal, no setup, no configuration — that's genuinely hard.

We hit every edge case. Dynamic imports that PyInstaller couldn't detect. DB paths that worked in dev but failed silently in production. CORS errors that only appeared in the packaged app because Windows uses a different WebView origin than macOS.

Every one of those failures made the product better. And every one of them will be documented so other builders don't lose the same time.

**What's next**

v0.1.0 is live. Windows only for now.

Here's the roadmap we're executing on:

1. **Web version** — same AI, same memory, accessible from any browser
2. **Proactive Solar** — push notifications, pattern surfacing, daily briefings
3. **Abstract the API key** — hosted relay so you sign in with email instead of managing API keys
4. **iPhone app** — your solar system in your pocket

We're opening limited beta spots. If you want early access, you're in the right place.

More next week — including the full technical decisions behind the memory system.

— Liam
Founder, Solar AI OS

P.S. If you know someone who would love this, forward it. Building in public means building with people.
```
